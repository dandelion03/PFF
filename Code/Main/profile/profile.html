Dune 1999
added by ACHRAF303

A lot can be said about this beautiful world-building by Frank Herbert. It had me thrilled from the start. This is truly an example of the sheer brilliance of a science fiction novel. The characters are super good in this book. The protagonist was a complex one that even his mother couldn't understand at times. The side characters were likeable too, each one of them having their own characteristics. The setting and complexities of the habitat in which Arrakis, the desert planet is set are commendable. Surely, it would have taken a lot of imagination to create something like this.

Kafka On The Shore 2002
added by ACHRAF303

✨ I had heard alot about Haruki Murakami and his writings , and wanted to read this one since a long time Finally I was able to complete this one , Kafka on the Shore ✨ Kafka, a 15 yrs old , trying to escape from his past towards his fate .. Nakata , is another character who holds the same value as Kafka.. his ability to look into the things carry an important value , brings an angle of mystery in this book . A very well written by Murakami.. ✨ Complex yet simple , this book is a masterpiece . Haruki Murakami's writting is indeed the best . ✨ How to look at things , at destiny , at past and at our future , is depicted through this . 2 different characters , 2 different lives , 2 different generations , 2 different thought process but similar at certain points . It's like 2 ends of the same thread 😃 ✨I totally appreciate the style of writing and highly recommend this book for all the bookworms out there ❤️
